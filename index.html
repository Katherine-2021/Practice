Katherine's Opinions on Astrological Signs

(As of May 2021)

Some people are really into zodiac signs, while others don't believe in them. I'm somewhere in between, but I thought it would be a fun topic to talk about. And by the way, do not take this personally, these opinions are generalizations. What I have really learned about people and zodiac signs is that the specific traits of a zodiac sign are often applicable to the person, but just because a person is a (x) zodiac sign, doens't mean that they automatically fit into that category. I understand that zodiac signs can give guidance and some grounding in life, but they don't have to make up who you are. This website groups my thoughts on the signs into their four element categories, which are:

  • Fire signs (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius)
  • Water signs (Virgo, Scorpio, Pisces)
  • Air signs (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius)
  • Earth signs (Tarus,Cancer, Capricorn)
